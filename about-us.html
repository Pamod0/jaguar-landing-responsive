Core Values

Honesty & integrity will be the cornerstones on which our relationships with our Clients and Employees are built: we will always be trusted to do the right thing.

Our purpose will always be beyond financial gain: service excellence and ethical employment will guide our decisions.

Relationships will always matter more: we will cultivate and maintain strong relationships with our Clients and Employees.

We will be valued and sought out by Clients and Employees: satisfied Clients and contended Employees will drive our efforts.

Timeless and perpetual: as an organisation, we will grow continuously and exponentially.

Our Mission

We are in the business of protecting people’s assets and interests. We prevent and solve crimes against our customers. We deploy knowledgeable and dedicated personnel who perform their duties to superior quality standards that meet and exceed customer expectations. We will be the leading private security and investigation service organisation in the Country.

Our Vision

We will change people’s perceptions of security and investigation services through innovation, personalised service, and commitment. We will become the benchmark for quality in security and investigation services in Sri Lanka. Our name will be synonymous with honesty, integrity and professionalism. We will have in our employment the most dedicated and contended personnel in the sector. We will grow continuously and exponentially. We will become the most respected and dependable security and investigation service provider in the Country, and the first choice of discerning Customers who identify with our core values.
