ABOUT US

Jaguar (Private) Limited is a Colombo, Sri Lanka, based private security and investigation service company, incorporated in July 1996. Our Board of Directors is headed by our Chairman and Managing Director, a retired senior Police officer and leading professional in the fields of Security, Law Enforcement, and Investigation, and comprises three other Business Administration professionals. We are experienced and knowledgeable in security and investigation services, and we are a trusted service provider to leading corporate clients and individuals in Sri Lanka and abroad. Our security service installations range from Apartments, Banks, Bungalows, Factories, Hotels, Offices, Restaurants, Schools, Shops & Showrooms, to Warehouses, while our investigation services are sought by almost all leading private sector companies, as well as overseas organisations with interests in this country.

We believe strongly in doing right, what we do. We are an ethical employer, and equally, an ethical service provider. Honesty and integrity are the cornerstones of all our dealings with our Clients and Employees. We are dependable and trustworthy. Our Employees believe in us, as do our Clients. We believe in building strong relationships that are fair and beneficial to all stakeholders. We can always be trusted to do the right thing. Our Employees are our only asset, and we invest in them. We cultivate loyalty and we foster commitment. We train for excellence and always strive to achieve it. We help develop their skills and competencies and we instill in them the right attitude. To our Clients, we epitomise trust. We practice transparency in our dealings, along with honest dialogue. We focus on service quality and continuous improvement. We bring to bear our considerable knowledge and experience in providing them protection. Safeguarding their interests and assets will remain our primary objective.

We recognise our role in the larger fabric of society. Our purpose is to serve, excellently, in our chosen field. We exist to protect our Clients and Employees, and thereby our community. We seek to change the perceptions of society through our exemplary service. We understand the need to be a good corporate citizen contributing our fair share to society, the Government, and the Country. We take pride in our responsibilities. We understand our strengths and weaknesses. We know where we are and where we want to go. We assess ourselves regularly, always seeking to improve, from a Client’s point of view. We will not be satisfied with our achievements, believing we can always do better. We will continue to change and grow in the coming years, as we have done in the past. We believe in stability and dependability. Our aim is to be the best-in-class security service provider; highly valued and sought out by both Clients and Employees. We will continue to be the benchmark of quality in the private security and investigation business.
