We are, and we have been, over the past several years, the number one private investigation company in the country, both in terms of volume and value of investigations carried out. We have broken new ground in handling reliable and confidential investigations for a wide cross section of clients, both local and overseas. Our prestigious clientele, comprising Banks, Insurance companies, Law firms, leading businesses, and individuals, are all repeat customers who continue to avail our services.

Investigation operations are conducted directly by the Managing Director, and the General Manager, another senior Police officer. All field officers are hand-picked retired Police officers, with notable intelligence service or crime investigation backgrounds. Field officers are recruited on the Managing Director’s personal knowledge of their service records, honesty, and integrity, in alignment with Company values.

The extensive intelligence and crime detection knowledge of the Managing Director, Management, and field officers is brought to bear on each case, maintaining the highest level of confidentiality and integrity. Liaison and communication with Law Enforcement officers and Government agencies are carried out where required, using the excellent relationships developed over time, to expedite investigations or prosecutions.

A concise yet comprehensive reporting system is maintained to communicate investigations and conclusions. All reports are once again personally verified by the Managing Director and carry his seal of authority. Depending on the duration of the operation, a systematic interim reporting format is followed, to ensure regular progress updates to the Client.

Fraud and crime investigation is carried out in a manner that facilitates prosecution at Client discretion. Authenticated evidence gathering is carried out, ensuring compliance with the law, and cases are ‘cracked’ using years of crime solving expertise, to ensure ‘watertight’ cases. The extensive law enforcement experience in leading evidence during trial is also made available to Clients, and where required, the Managing Director and the operatives make themselves available as specialist witnesses during prosecution.

The success of our investigation service depends on our uncompromising integrity; professional approach; intelligence and crime solving expertise; law enforcement expertise; honest, accurate and diligent investigation; legally compliant evidence gathering; professional reporting; trial experience; and prosecution witness capabilities.
